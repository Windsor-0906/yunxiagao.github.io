About Me

My name is Yunxia GAO, I'm from Beijing, the capital of China. I worked as an HR professional in China for 10 years.This is 1th year that I have been in Canada. My favorite sport is badminton. The exhilarating feeling it gives me makes me feel completely relaxed. Additionally, I also enjoy photography. I hope to capture the beautiful moments around me through the lens, and I especially love documenting my daughter's growth.

In fact, studying Interactive Media Design is truly challenging for me. However, I am really passionate about this field, and I need to learn a skill that will serve as a foundation for finding a job and staying in Canada.

The only way to do great work is to love what you do. --Steve Jobs

Although life is not easy for me right now, I still remain hopeful. I believe that the challenges are temporary, and everything will gradually get better.
